Teachers have a moral obligation to “tell it like it is” by basing the education of their students on facts supported by scientific evidence. On the basis of this obligation this resource is based on the clear and unequivocal scientific evidence that planet Earth is facing a climate emergency. The aim of this web site is to use the best possible scientific evidence to improve student understand and engage the next generation of Canadians and global citizens on the critical issue of climate change. The present and future citizens of any democratic country will face climate change. This will demand a basic level of scientific literacy to rational thinking of science in relation to personal, social, political and economic challenges. ( Wayne’s & Nicholas, Hurd). Climate change is a global issue with ramification in the personal, social, political and economic sphere of our life.
