Sketch examples of relations with the following domain and range. Use a Cartesian plane with the following scale $X:[-10,10,1]$ and $Y:[-10,10,1]$.

  1. $D: \{x|x\in\mathbb{R} \}$, $R: \{y|y\in\mathbb{R}\}$
  2. $D: \{x|x\geq 0, x \in \mathbb{R}\}$, $R: \{y|y\geq 0, y \in \mathbb{R}\}$
  3. $D: \{x|x\geq 0, x \in \mathbb{R}\}$, $R: \{y|y\in\mathbb{R}\}$
  4. $D: \{x|x \geq -2, x\in\mathbb{R}\}$, $\{y| y \leq -2, y\in\mathbb{R} \}$
  5. $D: (-\infty, \infty)$, $R: (-\infty, \infty) $
  6. $D: (-\infty, 0)$, $R: (-\infty, \infty)$
  7. $D: (-\infty, \infty)$, $R: [1, \infty)$
