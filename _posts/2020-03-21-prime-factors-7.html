A buoy is a floating device, usually anchored in a certain place in a lake or in the ocean. Buoys are often used to help ships navigate. The lights on one navigation buoy flash every 25 seconds, while the lights on another buoy flash every 32 seconds. What is the time interval between simultaneous flashes, i.e. how often (in seconds) do they flash together.
