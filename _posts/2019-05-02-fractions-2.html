Convert each of the mixed fractions below into an improper fraction and simplify to lowest terms.

  1. $$3\fraction{4}{6}$$
  2. $$1\fraction{8}{12}$$
  3. $$5\fraction{5}{10}$$
  4. $$17\fraction{2}{4}$$
{% capture solution %}
  1. $$3\fraction{4}{6} = \fraction{22}{6} = \fraction{11}{3}$$
  2. $$1\fraction{8}{12} = \fraction{20}{12} = \fraction{5}{3}$$
  3. $$5\fraction{5}{10} = \fraction{55}{10} = \fraction{11}{2}$$
  4. $$17\fraction{2}{4} = \fraction{70}{4} = \fraction{35}{2}$$
