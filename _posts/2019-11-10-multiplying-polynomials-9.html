Let $x$ represent any integer. Determine a simplified expression, in terms of $x$, for the...

  1. sum of three consecutive integers starting with $x$.
  2. sum of three consecutive integers ending with $x$.
  3. product of three consecutive integers starting with $x$.
  4. product of three consecutive integers ending with $x$.
  5. product of three consecutive even integers starting with $x$. Hint!
{% if false %}

A.

\[ \begin{align} & (9a+4)+(2a+2)+(a-4) \\ = & 9a+4+2a+2+a-4 \\ = & 12a + 2 \end{align} \]

B.

\[ \begin{align} 12a+2 & = 38 \\ 12a & = 36 \\ a & = 36 \div 12 \\ a & = 3 \end{align} \]
