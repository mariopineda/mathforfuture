Solve.

  1. $$4d = -\fraction{2}{5}$$
  2. $$\fraction{7}{5} = \fraction{y}{5}$$
  3. $$\fraction{7}{h} = -\fraction{2}{5}$$
  4. $$-7.6u = -15.2$$
  5. $$2\pi r = 2$$

{% capture solution %}

A.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} 4d & = & -\fraction{2}{5} \\ d & = & -\fraction{2}{5} \times 4 \\ d & = & -\fraction{8}{5} \end{array} \]

B.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} \fraction{y}{5} & = & \fraction{7}{5} \\ y & = & \fraction{7}{5} \times 5 \\ y & = & y \end{array} \]

C.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} -\fraction{2}{5} & = & \fraction{7}{h} \\ -\fraction{2}{5} \times h & = & 7 \\ h & = & -\fraction{5}{2} \times 7 \\ h & = & -\fraction{35}{2}& \end{array} \]

D.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} -7.6u & = & -15.2 \\ u & = & \fraction{-15.2}{-7.6} \\ u & = & 2 \\ \end{array} \]

E.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} 2\pi r & = & 2 \\ r & = & \fraction{2}{2\pi} & \\ r & = & \fraction{1}{\pi} \\ \end{array} \] {% endcapture %} {% include_relative solution.html content=solution %}
