  1. Using an appropriate regression function, determine the equation describing the height of the stone as a function of time. Round parameters to the nearest hundreth. Justify your choice of regression function.
  2. Determine the maximum height of the stone above the ground.
  3. Determine, to the nearest tenth of a second, the time it takes for the stone to hit the ground.
