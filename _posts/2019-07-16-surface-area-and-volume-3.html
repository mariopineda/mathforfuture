You have accepted to summer job of painting your classroom. Before you can start painting you need to determine the amount of paint that will be required and the cost of the paint. Each can of paint holds 3.79 litres of paint and covers 400 feet$^2$. The classroom needs two coats of paint.

  1. Determine the amount, rounded to the nearest litre, of paint that will be required to paint the classroom.
  2. How many cans of paint, rounded up to the nearest can, do you need to buy?
  3. If each can of paint costs $38.97, how much will all the paint cost?
