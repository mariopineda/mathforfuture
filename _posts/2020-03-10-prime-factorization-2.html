Using prime factorization, determine if the following numbers are perfect squares, perfect cube, neither or both.

  1. 1225
  2. 3042
  3. 44100
  4. 6612
  5. 970299
  6. 27000
{% capture solution %}
  1. $1225 = 5^2 \times 7^2$
  2. $3042 = 2 \times 3^2 \times 13^2$
  3. $44100 = 2^2 \times 3^2 \times 5^2 \times 7^2$
  4. $6612 = 2^2 \times 3 \times 19 \times 29$
  5. $970299 = 3^6 \times 11^3$
  6. $27000 = 2^3 \times 3^3 \times 5^3$
