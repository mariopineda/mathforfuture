  1. A triangle has side lengths of $9a+4$, $2a+2$ ans $a-4$.
    1. Determine an expression for the perimeter of this triangle.
    2. If the perimeter of this triangle is 38 cm, determine the value of $a$.
  2. A triangle has side lengths of $2b+1$, $3b-1$ and a perimeter of $13b^2+3b+2$. Determine the expression for the length of the third side.
  3. In $\triangle ABC$, $AB = AC$, $BC=b+1$ and $AB+AC+BC=3b+5$. Determine an expression for AB.
{% if false %}

A.

\[ \begin{align} & (9a+4)+(2a+2)+(a-4) \\ = & 9a+4+2a+2+a-4 \\ = & 12a + 2 \end{align} \]

B.

\[ \begin{align} 12a+2 & = 38 \\ 12a & = 36 \\ a & = 36 \div 12 \\ a & = 3 \end{align} \]
