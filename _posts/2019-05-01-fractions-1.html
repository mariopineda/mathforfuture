Convert each of the improper fractions below into a decimal to nearest hundreth, a mixed fraction and find an equivalent fraction that has a denominator of 24.

  1. $$\fraction{15}{6}$$
  2. $$\fraction{7}{2}$$
  3. $$\fraction{100}{48}$$
  4. $$\fraction{48}{36}$$
{% capture solution %}
    1. Decimal: $$\fraction{15}{6} \approx 2.50$$, Mixed fraction: $$\fraction{15}{6} = 2\fraction{3}{6} = 2\fraction{1}{2}$$, Equivalent fraction: $$\fraction{15}{6} = \fraction{60}{24}$$
    2. $$\fraction{7}{2}$$
    3. $$\fraction{100}{48}$$
    4. $$\fraction{48}{36}$$
