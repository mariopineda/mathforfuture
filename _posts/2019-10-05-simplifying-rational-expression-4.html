Simplify the following rational expressions.

  1. $$\fraction{6x}{(x-4)^2} - \fraction{2}{x-4}$$
  2. $$\fraction{3}{x-7} - \fraction{4}{x^2-49}$$
  3. $$\fraction{4}{x-3} - \fraction{2}{3-x}$$
  4. $$\fraction{7x(x-3)}{(x+7)(x+2)} \div \fraction{21x(x-3)}{(x+7)^2}$$
  5. $$\left( 6-\fraction{2}{x} \right) \div \left( 9 - \fraction{1}{x^2} \right)$$
