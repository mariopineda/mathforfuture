For each scenario:

  • Create a table of values between $x_{min}=-10$, $x_{max}=10$, $y_{min}=-10$ and $y_{max}=10$
  • Graph the relations
  • Identify relation as linear or non-linear
  • Identify the x and y-intercepts
  • Verify your answer using a graphing calculator

  1. $y=4-x$
  2. $y=x^2+3x-5$
  3. $y=\displaystyle\frac{1}{4}x$
  4. $y=(x+3)(x-2)$
  5. $-3y+2x=9$
