Sketch a graph which best illustrates the given scenario. For each scenario:

  • Identify the dependent and independent variables.
  • Describe the graph of the scenario in words.
  • Label axes
  • Identify units

  1. How a person's heigh above ground varies over time during a Ferris wheel ride lasting three revolutions.
  2. Number of jumps made by a frog and the total distance it travels.
  3. Height of bouncy ball after being dropped from the top of a building
  4. Outdoor temperature over a 24 hour period.
  5. Altitude as you bike up and down a hill.
  6. Depth as you dive into a pool from a diving board. The water surface is at 0 metres height.
  7. Your speed as you bike up and down a hill.
