In 2015 the provincial government in Alberta increased the minimum wage for students under 18 to 15 dollars/hour. In 2019 the newly elected provincial government rolled back the minimum salary to 13 dollars/hour.

A.

Express these relations using equations, where $E$ represent total earnings and $h$ represents the number of hours worked.

B.

Graph the relations (on the same graph using two colours, one for each equation) for a 40 hour work week. Give the graph a title and label the axes. State the independent and dependent variables.

C.

Should the data values be joined by a continuous line? Explain your answer.

D.

What would your earnings be for an 40 hour work week under the two pay rates?

E.

What is the difference in earnings between the two pay rates? How much less would you earn after a week after the roll back?

F.

How many hours would you have to work to earn $1000 under the two pay rates?
