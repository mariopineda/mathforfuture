Evaluate without a calculator.

  1. $$\fraction{3}{12} + \fraction{5}{12}$$
  2. $$\fraction{11}{6} + \fraction{1}{3}$$
  3. $$\fraction{19}{12} - \fraction{4}{3}$$
  4. $$\fraction{14}{4} - \fraction{8}{6}$$
  5. $$\fraction{3}{4} + \fraction{1}{16} - \fraction{6}{8}$$
  6. $$\fraction{4}{11} + \fraction{3}{8} - \fraction{15}{22}$$

  1. $$\fraction{3}{12} + \fraction{5}{12} = \fraction{8}{12} = \fraction{2}{3}$$
  2. $$\fraction{11}{6} + \fraction{1}{3} = \fraction{11}{6} + \fraction{2}{6} = \fraction{13}{6}$$
  3. $$\fraction{19}{12} - \fraction{4}{3}$$
  4. $$\fraction{14}{4} - \fraction{8}{6}$$
  5. $$\fraction{3}{4} + \fraction{1}{16} - \fraction{6}{8}$$
  6. $$\fraction{4}{11} + \fraction{3}{8} - \fraction{15}{22}$$
