On 10 January 1992, during a storm in the North Pacific Ocean a shipping container was washed overboard from the container ship the Ever Laurel. One of these containers held 28,800 Floatees, a child's bath toy which came in a number of forms: red beavers, green frogs, blue turtles and yellow ducks.

  1. A standard shipping container measures $3x+5$ metres by $(2x-3)$ metres by $(2x+4)$ metres. Determine which expression represents the length, width and height. Hint!
  2. Determine a simplified expression for the volume of a shipping container.
  3. Determine a simplified expression for the surface area of a shipping container.
  4. If $x=\displaystyle\frac{12}{5}$, determine the dimensions of a shipping container.

A.

\[ \begin{align} & (9a+4)+(2a+2)+(a-4) \\ = & 9a+4+2a+2+a-4 \\ = & 12a + 2 \end{align} \]

B.

\[ \begin{align} 12a+2 & = 38 \\ 12a & = 36 \\ a & = 36 \div 12 \\ a & = 3 \end{align} \]
