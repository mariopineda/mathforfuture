Convert the following unit as indicated, rounded to the nearest hundredth when applicable.

  1. $26 \mbox{ mm} \Rightarrow \mbox{cm}$
  2. $8.174 \mbox{ km} \Rightarrow \mbox{m}$
  3. $1.6 \mbox{ cm} \Rightarrow \mbox{m}$
  4. $572 \mbox{ mm} \Rightarrow \mbox{m}$
  5. $5 \mbox{ m} \Rightarrow \mbox{mm}$
  6. $98 \mbox{ m} \Rightarrow \mbox{km}$
