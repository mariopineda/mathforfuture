  1. Determine, to the nearest whole degree, the measure of $\angle C$ in $\triangle ABC$ where $c=4$ cm, $b=6$ cm and $\angle A = 90^\circ$.
  2. Determine, to the nearest tenth, the measure of side $r$ in $\triangle PQR$ where $\angle P = 20^\circ$, $p=8$ cm and $q$ is the longest side.

{% capture solution %}

A.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} \tan{\angle C} & = & \fraction{4}{6} \\ \angle C & = & \taninv\fraction{4}{6} \\ \angle C & = & 33.69^\circ \ldots \approx 34^\circ \end{array} \] {% endcapture %} {% include_relative solution.html content=solution %}
