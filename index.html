Welcome to Math For Future

This website is a collection of high school math practice problems aligned with the Alberta Education High School Program of Study in Mathematics.

Due to the recent school closure caused by the COVID-19 pandemic I have made this website avaliable to the general public. My hope is that this resource will be helpful to, in particular, high school students in Alberta as they are finishing their school year from home.

May the math be with you,

Dr. P.
