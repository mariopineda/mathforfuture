Abbreviations

CD concave downward
CU concave upward
D the domain of $f$
DoC Difference of Cubes, $A^3-B^3$
DoS Difference of Squares, $A^2-B^2$
FbG Factor by Grouping
GCF Greatest Common Factor
HA horizontal asymptote(s)
IP inflection point
LCD Lowest Common Denominator
LCM Lowest Common Multiple
LHS left-hand side
MoD Method of Decomposition
R the range of $f$
RHS right-hand side
SuC Sum of Cubes, $A^3+B^3$
VA vertical asymptote(s)

Symbols

$\parallel$parallel, e.g. $x \parallel y$ means $x$ is parallel to $y$
$\nparallel$not parallel, e.g. $x \nparallel y$ means $x$ is not parallel to $y$
$\perp$perpendicular, e.g. $x \perp y$ means $x$ is perpendicular to $y$
$\cong$is congruent to, e.g. $x \cong y$ means $x$ is congruent to $y$
$\ncong$is not congruent to, e.g. $x \ncong y$ means $x$ is not congruent to $y$
$\triangle$triangle
$\angle$angle
$\overline{\rm AB}$line segement connecting vertices $A$ and $B$
$\infty$positive infinity
$-\infty$negative infinity
$\cup$union
$\cap$intersection
$\subset$subset
$\not\subset$not a subset
$\approx$approximatelly
$\neq$not equal to
