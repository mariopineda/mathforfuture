Exam Weights
The exams are weighted as follows:
  • Unit Finals: 10%
  • Final Exam: 20%
Reassessment of Learning Outcomes
Students who complete 90% of assigned class work and were unable to demonstrate their understanding of 1 or 2 learning outcomes on a unit test will be provided a reasessment opportunity on the practice test day following the unit final to attempt the 1 or 2 learning outcomes again. Students wanting to get reassessed must demostrate a 90% assignment completion and make the request in person to their teacher no later than 24-hours prior to the reassessment opportunity.
Replacement Exams
Replacement Exams provide an opportunity for students to re-challenge previously evaluated outcomes. Replacement exams are offered during the course on the dates indicated in the timeline. To qualify for a replacement exam a student must have an assignment completion of at least 90% in all the units the replacement exam covers. Students that qualify may request a replacement exam through the class website.
Missed Exams
You are expected to write tests on the scheduled dates. Failure to meet this expectation will result in a referral to the student support team or to the administration. There are no late writes on replacement exams and reassessments.
